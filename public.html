The Platform

Building a greener,
more equitable world

The crown is a microphone. I intend to use it to amplify the communities who have been engineered out of prosperity — and to build real solutions alongside them.

01
☀

Green Energy Access

Solar panels, microgrids, and renewable infrastructure built in and for underserved communities. Not charity — engineering. Real partnerships with municipalities, nonprofits, and clean energy companies to bring power to the people who need it most.

02
⚲

Community Infrastructure

Where energy alone isn’t the answer, we engineer the solution that is. Clean water systems, connectivity infrastructure, sustainable housing materials — the platform adapts to what the community actually needs, built with their voices at the table.

03
⛀

Fashion Sustainability Policy

Fashion is the second most polluting industry on earth. It doesn’t have to be. I’m pushing for transparency legislation, recycled textile requirements, and supply chain accountability — from runway to regulation.

About Amelia

Engineer. Artist.
Cellist. Builder.

I grew up believing that the pursuit of knowledge and the pursuit of happiness are the same pursuit. I’m a senior software engineer, co-founder, oil painter, and cellist — and I move through the world convinced that human creations are most beautiful when the process behind them cultivates healthy human connections.

I build systems that breathe. At Ballmecca and across the teams I’ve led, I’ve learned that the best infrastructure — technical or human — is the kind that holds space for people to fully show up. That’s the leadership I bring to this campaign.
